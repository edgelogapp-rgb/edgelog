Overview

Our Privacy Commitment

EdgeLog ("we," "our," or "us") is a professional trade journaling application purpose-built for serious traders and investors. We understand that your trading activity represents some of your most sensitive financial data, and we treat it accordingly.

Core Principle

Your trade records are stored exclusively on your device. EdgeLog does not transmit, sell, rent, or monetise your personal trading data — in any form, to anyone, ever.

This Privacy Policy applies to the EdgeLog Android application (v1.0) and the website at edgelog.app. By using EdgeLog, you consent to the practices described herein. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App immediately.

03
Data Use

How We Use Your Data

Information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Delivering core app functionality — trade logging, P&L analytics, and performance insights
  • Verifying and managing your EdgeLog Pro subscription via Google Play Billing
  • Diagnosing crashes and technical issues to maintain a stable, high-quality experience
  • Understanding aggregate, anonymised feature usage to guide product improvements
  • Responding to support queries and grievance requests you submit directly to us

We do not use your data for targeted advertising. We do not build marketing profiles. We do not sell or license insights derived from your usage to any third party.

04
Security

Storage & Security

EdgeLog is built on a local-first architecture. Your trade journals never leave your device unless you explicitly export them via CSV.

  • App sandbox: All trade data is stored within Android's isolated, encrypted app storage — inaccessible to other applications
  • TLS 1.3: All network communications (crash reports, analytics) are encrypted in transit
  • No cloud storage: We do not operate servers that hold your personal trading records
  • Auto-purge: All diagnostic data is automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Zero ad networks: We integrate no advertising SDKs or data broker pipelines
Important Notice

Protect your device with a PIN, pattern, or biometric lock. If your device is lost, factory-reset, or the App is uninstalled, locally stored trade data cannot be recovered by EdgeLog. Use the CSV export feature regularly as a backup.

Your Rights

Your Rights & Choices

Under applicable Indian law, you hold the following rights with respect to your data:

  • Access: Request a summary of any personal data we hold about you beyond on-device storage
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate personal information in our systems
  • Deletion: Uninstalling EdgeLog removes all on-device data automatically. For diagnostic data, contact our Grievance Officer
  • Portability: Export all trade data at any time via the in-app CSV export in a machine-readable format
  • Analytics opt-out: Disable ad personalisation via Android Settings → Google → Ads
  • Grievance: Lodge a formal complaint with our Grievance Officer — guaranteed resolution within 30 days

To exercise any right above, contact our Grievance Officer (Section 09). All requests are acknowledged within 3 business days.
